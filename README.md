# 🏙 Bengaluru OS

## AI Powered Urban Intelligence Platform


Bengaluru OS is an AI-driven city intelligence platform that analyzes Bengaluru using Data Science, Machine Learning and Generative AI.


## Problem

Bengaluru faces challenges in:

- Traffic congestion
- Housing decisions
- Career opportunities
- Urban growth
- Infrastructure planning


## Solution

A unified AI platform that provides:

- Area intelligence
- Housing analytics
- Traffic insights
- Metro connectivity
- Career recommendations
- Future city prediction


# Features


## 🏙 Area Intelligence

Analyze Bengaluru locations using multiple city factors.


## 🚦 Traffic Intelligence

Traffic analysis and forecasting.


## 🌦 Weather Intelligence

Weather-based area insights.


## 🚇 Metro Intelligence

Connectivity analysis.


## 🏠 Housing Intelligence

Rent analytics and prediction.


## 💼 Career Intelligence

Job and skill intelligence.


## 📈 Future Trends

Predict future Bengaluru growth areas.


## 🤖 AI Assistant

Ask Bengaluru OS about city decisions.
